Mission

This site provides a growing online collection of pedagogical resources that faculty who teach with digital resources can use in the courses that they teach.

Originally designed to support a learning community of faculty in the Virginia Tech Department of English who teach courses online and who use digital resources in campus classrooms, this public collection allows teachers in all departments and at other colleges and universities to take advantage of the resources.

To facilitate use by the entire learning community, all materials on the site are shared under a Creative Commons Attribution-NonCommercial Share-Alike 4.0 International License, unless otherwise noted.

Support

This site was created with support of a High-Impact Project Grant from the Virginia Tech Center for Teaching and Learning.

Colophon

This site is hosted on GitHub, using GitHub Pages on a custom domain. Icons from Font Awesome and The Noun Project Pro. Site search uses a custom Google Search, and the site announcements are managed in a Google Group. The site uses Bootstrap, jQuery, and HTML5/CSS.
