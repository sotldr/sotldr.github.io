Mission

The mission of the site is to build an online collection of tutorials, demos, teaching tips, and classroom materials that faculty who teach with digital resources can tap for planning and classroom use.

This site was originally designed as part of a project to support faculty in the Virginia Tech Department of English who teach courses online and who use digital resources in campus classrooms. By posting the materials publically, teachers in all departments and at other colleges and universities can take advantage of the collection. All resources on the site are shared under Creative Commons licensing.
