Mission

This site provides a growing online collection of pedagogical resources that faculty who teach with digital resources can use in the courses that they teach.

Originally designed to support a learning community of faculty in the Virginia Tech Department of English who teach courses online and who use digital resources in campus classrooms, this public collection allows teachers in all departments and at other colleges and universities to take advantage of the resources. All materials on the site are shared under Creative Commons licensing, unless otherwise noted.
